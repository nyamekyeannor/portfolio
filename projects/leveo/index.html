About Leveo

Leveo is an AI-powered Chrome extension that acts as a job search command center. It lives in your browser as a side panel and lets job seekers track applications, score their resume against job listings, and draft personalized outreach — all without leaving LinkedIn, Indeed, or Greenhouse.

My Role

Brought on as Leveo's Developer Advocate to build awareness and demonstrate the product to a real audience. I produce a 12-video content series distributed across YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok — showing real people how Leveo fits into their actual job search. Not scripted demos, but genuine content that developers and job seekers can relate to and act on.

Deliverables

  • 12-video content series (in production)
  • Distribution across YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok
  • Product walkthroughs and feature breakdowns
  • Lifestyle content showing Leveo in a real job search context
